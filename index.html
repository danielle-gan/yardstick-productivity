The 3-3-3 Rule

About the 3-3-3 Rule

Welcome to the 3-3-3 Rule, a productivity framework that can transform the way you work and accomplish your most important tasks. In a world filled with distractions and time constraints, finding an approach that helps you maintain focus and balance is crucial. The 3-3-3 Rule offers a structured way to tackle your daily to-do list and prioritize your tasks effectively.

What is it?

The 3-3-3 Rule, revolves around the number three, which signifies a balanced approach to task management. By dividing your day or tasks into sets of three, you can effectively focus on key areas without becoming overwhelmed.

Variations

Work Focused

  • 3 hours to your most important task
  • 3 shorter, sticky tasks
  • 3 maintenance tasks

Goal Focused

  • 3 main goals
  • 3 specific tasks for each goal
  • 3 minutes of planning

Well-being focused

  • 3 hours of relaxation
  • 3 physical activities
  • 3 mindful reflections

Why Choose the 3-3-3 Rule?

The 3-3-3 Rule offers you the freedom to select the version that best aligns with your daily goals, work style, and well-being. It's a dynamic approach to task management that promotes balance and efficiency in various aspects of your life, from work to leisure, and everything in between
